# QM640 Data Analytics Capstone

## Topic
Determinants of Urban Air Quality in Indian Cities: Evaluating the Effects of Smart City Status, Electric Vehicle Adoption, Green Cover, and Population Density Using Statistical and Machine Learning Approaches.

## Objectives
- Evaluate the impact of Smart City status on AQI.
- Assess the effect of EV adoption on AQI.
- Examine the relationship between green cover and AQI.
- Investigate the influence of population density on AQI.

## Dataset
